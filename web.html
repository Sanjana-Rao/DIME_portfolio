Web Design

This dynamic drag-and-drop quiz showcases my proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The quiz immerses users in a fun, interactive experience where they match items, locations, or characters to their respective series or fandom. The design reflects a deep magical theme, using visually appealing graphics and an intuitive drag-and-drop interface. The goal is to test the participant's knowledge of their favorite fandoms while providing an enjoyable and user-friendly experience. Users match famous fictional locations to their respective series, where each location must be dragged and dropped into a corresponding series box (e.g., The Lord of the Rings, Batman). Once a user drops an item, the quiz provides immediate feedback, such as highlighting correct or incorrect matches, prompting users to try again for incorrect attempts.
