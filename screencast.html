Screencasting

This video is a quick tutorial that demonstrates three productivity features in Google Chrome to enhance the browsing experience. It starts by showing how to explore organizing tabs effectively using tab groups, followed by an introduction to Chrome extensions like Grammarly to improve writing accuracy. Next, it shows how to manage bookmarks for easy access to our favorite websites. With clear narration, practical examples, and panning/highlighting effects, this video equips viewers with tools to browse smarter and faster.
